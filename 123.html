Daily Health Tips

Simple habits for more energy

  1. Hydrate first thing: Drink a glass of water right after waking up.
  2. Move your body: Aim for 20–30 minutes of walking or light exercise.
  3. Eat colorful: Add at least 2 servings of veggies to your meals.
  4. Guard your sleep: Keep a consistent 7–9 hour schedule.
  5. Take screen breaks: 20-20-20 rule—every 20 minutes, look 20 ft away for 20 seconds.

Tip: Start with one new habit this week and build from there.
